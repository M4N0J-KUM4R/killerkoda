# Tour Complete.

**Summary:**
- **Bridge**: Default. Good for 90% of apps.
- **Host**: Good for performance / networking tools.
- **None**: Good for secure batch jobs.
- **Overlay**: **Essential** for Swarm/Kubernetes.
- **Macvlan**: Legacy apps requiring physical IPs.
- **IPvlan**: High performance, handles massive scale better than Macvlan.
